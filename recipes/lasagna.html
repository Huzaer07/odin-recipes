A recipe to make a tasty lasagna!

Description

Lasagna,also known by the plural form lasagne, is a type of pasta made in wide, flat sheets. It originates in Italian cuisine, where it is served in a number of ways, including in broth (lasagne in brodo), but is best known for its use in a baked dish made by stacking layers of pasta.

Ingredients

  • Tomato sauce (crushed/paste)
  • Ground meat (beef/sausage)
  • Cheese (mozzarella, ricotta/cottage, Parmesan)
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Flavourings:onion, garlic, herbs (basil/parsley/oregano),black pepper,salt
